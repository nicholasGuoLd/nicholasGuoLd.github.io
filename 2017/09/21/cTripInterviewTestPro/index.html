A Valuable Question about Product Manager

  • As a product manager
  • Analyze the position and market

    Choose 5 possible functions:

    After one magic dream, your boss and you (product manager) go back to the 1990. Your boss asks you to design one special chatting software. After he demostrate what he wants to achieve, you say you can just finish five function in his choice range. Please choose which five function do you need to develop and give the priority of each of them. The important thing is explain why you think it’s important and give your boss analysis.

    1. System security; 2. Chatting; 3. Picture delivering ; 4. Voice message; 5. Video chatting; 6. Small insert program; 7. Chatting history; 8. Personal interface; 9. Self-defination portrait; 10. Friends online mention;

My analysis:
In 1990, the development of Internet is the highest stage in the 20 century. More and more Internet products came out to people’s lives. For instance, Baidu, Alibaba and Tencent are the famous companies during that time. In this age, I will consider them as following aspects:

  1. System security(5 pts): As an Internet company, the most important thing is System security. If we do not have a system security, our customers’ personal detail will be lost and the chatting record also be missed. Protecting everyone’s data is the key of one Internet company’s development. Even I have already consider this software in business use.

  2. Chatting(4 pts): Of course, this is a chatting software, so chatting dunction is the core technology in our company’s strategy. In 1990, the number of mobile phones is not very popular and most of us used beeper as message communicating method. So, the chatting software is one innovation in that age.

  3. Picture delivering(3 pts): Here I choose picture but not video chatting or voice chatting, because the development of Internet is not strong like 30 years later. Although the quality of Internet is bad, the cost of Internet is one big part in people’s lives. So, even you allow them to use video chatting function, they cannot afford the high Internet fee. But pictures delivering is one of important function in Usability Engineering. A picture can contains too much information and it can give users a obvious sense. This is a great media of message delivering.

  4. Chatting history(2 pts): In term of chatting history, I separate it into 2 parts. One is about normal users that they use history record to check what they had said and what important information they need to save. On the other hand, businees is one of our benefits. This software cannot only consider about the normal customer, but also need to think about the business model. One product’s mission is not only increase the reputation of one company, but also need to make benefits for us. Companies will use this software as a communication proof or evidence in cooperation between two companies. At the same time, I will develop one professional edition for Business customers.

  5. Self-defination portrait(1 pts): As the mention of this question, our software is a special one. Slef-defination portrait is a particular way to distinguish with others. Personal interface is just a looklike in users’ own eyes, however, self-portrait is a different symble to represent youself.

To be a product manager, how to make it is not only one thing you need to consider, more about the big scale is a good standing point. Furthermore, benefits are the key to keep your project or product moving.
